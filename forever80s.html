Forever 80s

A case study of a music player app.

Objective

To research, create and design a music player app to meet the critera of:

  • Making a playlist
  • Ability to search for a specific artist
  • Creating a user profile

Competitive Analysis

Checking out the competition of Spotify and YouTube Music, I saw that I had my work cut out for me if I wanted to add features that weren't offered by the big dogs.

Layouts

I wanted to see how they were both laid out on desktop before starting to draw my own version.

Color Palette

The 80's had a wide variety of colors to choose from. Ranging from soft muted tones to vibrant neon, to the darker colors of the synth-wave vibe.

Choosing a Font

Ahh, fonts. One could get lost in a rabbit hole if they're not careful here. I researched fonts for the 80's until I settled on one that I felt fit the tone of the app, while keeping readability high. Futura.
