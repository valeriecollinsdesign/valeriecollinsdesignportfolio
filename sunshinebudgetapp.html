Sunshine Budget and Investments

You're not taught about finances and investments as a kid. You're left on your own to figure this stuff out. As a woman, that can be scary. I wanted to create an app that took the scariness out and empowered women to make smart financial descions.

Skills:

Case Study for Sunshine Budget and Investments

Women under earn, outlive and handle the majority of house hold finance. Yet know little about money, how it actually works, and how to ensure financial security for themselves. What gives? Well, it's imtimidating. It's not taught and it's been predominatly male-focused. I wanted to create a design focused on helping women empowering themselves financially.
