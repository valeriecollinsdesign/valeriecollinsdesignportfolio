The Zen Gardener

It's a proven fact that gardening relieves stress. So, why not make your own meditation garden? This app is designed to help users find their flow with gardening by helping users build their own meditaion garden in whatever space that is available.

Tools Used:

  • Balsamiq
  • Figma

Skills:

  • Critical Thinking
  • Active Listening
  • Lean UX Principals
  • Responsive Web Design
