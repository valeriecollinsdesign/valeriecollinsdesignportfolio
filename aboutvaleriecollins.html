About Me

Who I Am

Hello! I am former Service Advisor with multiple accrediations to my name (Accredited Master Automotive Manager and ASE certified to name a few). I worked my way up from parts driver to accredited Service Advisor over the course of a few years. I devoted to improving myself as an advisor so I would be able to serve my customers and technicians more effectively. Through the accreditdation process I learned about effect customer service, how to run a project successfully and ethical buisness practices.

Though I enjoyed advocating for my customers and helping to successfully run a shop, at the end of the day I craved to explore my creativity. As a side hobby I have explored photography, with an emphasis on iphone photography as the camera technology has become increasingly advanced. While that satiated that creative outlet for a bit, I still wanted more. I wanted to build something and contribute in a meaningful way. Enter UI design with a specialization in Front End Design. Through this medium I am able to create and contribute in a meaningful way. In addition, I will also utilize my skillset for effective project management and ethical buisness practices.

When I am not designing, you can find me continuing the life-long practice of yoga and mindfulness. The yoga and mindfulness helps center me for the mountain ultra marathons I enjoy doing. I enjoy the grit it takes to complete them and the exposure to nature refreshes me! When I am not practicing yoga or running in the mountains, I am often traveling with my family exploring new places and meeting new people!
